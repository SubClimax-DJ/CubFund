How It Works

Three Simple Steps to Start Raising Funds

1

School Signs Up

The school admin creates an account and sets up fundraising projects. Funds are tracked by the platform, and schools can cash out anytime.

2

Students Get Their Links

The admin adds students to a campaign. Each student gets a unique, shareable donation link they can send to family, friends, and supporters.

3

Donors Contribute

Donors click the link, choose one of the predefined donation amounts, and check out securely via Stripe. Every donation is tracked automatically to the student!
